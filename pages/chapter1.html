Enigma Fanfic Chapter 1/31 "Introduction"

The Enigma Fanfic starts right here, right now.


Outside campus 7:40AM

It was a cold winter morning dawning on Enigma High. Everyone has just come back from their holiday break. I think I can see my old friend, Namwen from a distance, walking into the school building...

I think i see... Someone else by his side... I started to walk a little bit faster to get a closer look of who he's with. As i reached the front of the school building. Namwen and this other person was walking upstairs to the 2nd floor.

I quickly followed them up and being sneaky like a ninja. They seem to be having quite a conversation while walking into the library... Maybe I can casually show up to meet him and his friend.

Library 8:00AM

I walked into the library looking around for Namwen and I've finally spotted him. As i walked toward him, he noticed me and said, "Hey, Butler! How's it going man! Did you have a good break?"

I responded with, "Yeah man! It's been good. Thanks for asking. How about yours?" Namwen replied with, "Good, good. Hey, have you met my new friend, Konmei ?"

"Oh! Hi there Konmei ! Are you new here? I havent seen you around this school much." Konmei responded with, "Yeah, I Just transfered into this school yesterday and Namwen here is showing me around the school."

"Oh, nice. *bell rings* Well I guess we should be going into class now. I'll see you guys later.", I said, "Okay, see ya." "Later.", they replied.

Classroom #General 8:30AM

Once the late bell has rung, our homeroom teacher was coming in with someone. I think it's Konmei since he's the only transfer student that I know of. Instead it was someone else with her...

The teacher said, "Okay, young man introduce yourself to the classroom." "Hi everyone! My name is Dado and I'm looking forward to being friends with you all!" He seemed like a nice guy.

Once we got our break, I approched Dado and we really got along well. I asked him if he wanted to be my friend and he said, "Wow! a new friend already!? And it's my first day here!"

Before the break ended. We exchanged numbers and decided to meet up at a local arcade after school.

Lunch 10:50AM

I'm glad my mom always makes me lunchboxes so I don't get trampled in the lunchroom line. While I was on my phone and eating... Someone else has come to my table and sat down.

He introduced himself to me and said his name was Ser.net. He was an ok guy but, he asked if he wanted to be my friend. So i said, "Sure why not." His eyes lit up for a bit and then he left the table.

Classroom #troubleshooting 1:30PM

Heading into my next class the teacher assigned us seats and I was assigned to sit down with a new student. My friend from way back texted me saying "You're going to love this dude."

I was confused of what he's saying but, the teacher said to the class to look at the front because the new student is coming in... The guy ran in and I couldn't believe my eyes...

My very first friend from my childhood Kacklappen. He introduced himself to the class and started to boldly walk to my desk where he and I were assigned to.

"Long time no see, dude! Why didn't you tell me you were moving back here?", I said, "I wanted it to be a surprise man! I've missed you so much after all these years.", he replied.

"Hey, I'm actually going to an arcade with another friend of mine after school. Do you want to tag along with me?" "Sure! why not!." I had a huge smile on my face and then the class started afterward

After school 3:00PM

I texted Dado about Kacklappen joining us to goto the arcade and he was more than glad to have him tag along with us.
